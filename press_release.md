### New Open Dataset Published: Comprehensive dataset of key health indicators for Colombia (2020-2025). Includes mental health metrics (depression, anxiety, suicide rates), healthcare workforce data, infectious disease surveillance (COVID-19, dengue, malaria, HIV), non-communicabl

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Comprehensive dataset of key health indicators for Colombia (2020-2025). Includes mental health metrics (depression, anxiety, suicide rates), healthcare workforce data, infectious disease surveillance (COVID-19, dengue, malaria, HIV), non-communicabl*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
